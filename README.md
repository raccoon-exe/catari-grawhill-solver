# Catari GrawHill Solver

This extension automates answering questions on McGraw Hill by leveraging AI models. 

## Installation & Usage Instructions

1. **Download and Extract**: Download the extension as a ZIP file and extract (unzip) it to a folder on your computer.
2. **Open Browser**: Open Google Chrome or (preferably) the Brave browser.
3. **Navigate to Extensions**: Go to your browser's extension settings (e.g., `chrome://extensions/` or `brave://extensions/`).
4. **Enable Developer Mode**: Turn on the "Developer mode" toggle, typically found in the top right corner.
5. **Load Extension**: Click on the **"Load unpacked"** button and select the folder where you extracted the extension.
6. **Open McGraw Hill**: Navigate to your McGraw Hill assignment page.
7. **Open AI Chat**: Open your preferred AI chatbot in another tab or window (ChatGPT, Gemini, or DeepSeek).
8. **Start Solving**: Click the **Start** button provided by the extension to begin!
